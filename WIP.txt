Work In Progress Notes - uc80 C Compiler
=========================================

Current Status: 219/220 c-testsuite tests passing

Recent Fixes:
- Fixed negative constant comparison bug in AST optimizer constant folding
  Unary minus and bitwise NOT folded values like -(1) to 65535 (masked unsigned)
  instead of -1 (signed). _is_long_expr saw 65535 > 32767 and promoted to 32-bit,
  where zero-extension (0x0000FFFF) mismatched sign-extended variables (0xFFFFFFFF).
  This caused infinite loops in fgetc() != EOF and other negative comparisons.
  Fixed both unary and binary constant folding to convert results back to signed.
  Tests 00187, 00200, 00216, 00220 now passing (were failing before).
